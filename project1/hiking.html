Ice climbing & hiking on glaciers

Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike on Solhemajokull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is availble all year around. The tour offers a pick-up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Solheimajokull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in iceland and come explore more......
